## Contributing

The Nobar Charity Institute welcomes collaboration in the form of volunteering, donations, and spreading awareness. To contribute:

1. Register as a volunteer via the (volunteer).
2. Share campaigns on social media.
3. Provide feedback through the contact form.

Financial and in-kind contributions are always appreciated and directly impact lives.
